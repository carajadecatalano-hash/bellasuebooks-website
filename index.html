About Bella Sue

Sweet stories for little dreamers 🌙

Bella Sue is a mother and aunt to a niece and nephew — and a former preschool teacher in San Bernardino County with years of experience with young children. She has published several children's fantasy books for parents to read to their toddlers, preschoolers, and other young children. She enjoys bringing colorful digital art and rhyming poetry to help young readers get excited about books.

All books available on Amazon in ebook and paperback.
