About Bella Sue

The person behind the picture books.

Sweet stories for little dreamers 🌙

Bella Sue is a mother and aunt to a niece and nephew — and a former preschool teacher at Nadia's Montessori Childcare in Chino, California with years of experience with young children. She has published several children's fantasy books for parents to read to their toddlers, preschoolers, and other young children. With a teacher's wisdom and a mother's heart, she brings colorful digital art and rhyming poetry to young readers to get them excited about books.

The Sweet Bedtime Stories series is packed with AABB rhymes, bright fantastical worlds, and the kind of cozy warmth that makes bedtime something to look forward to. Perfect for ages 0–6.

The My Mommy Is A... series celebrates moms in careers that break barriers — police officers, firefighters, pilots, scientists — told from a child's point of view with real career vocabulary and strong, loving characters.

All books are available on Amazon in ebook and paperback.
